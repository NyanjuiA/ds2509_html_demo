HTML Lists

A list is a collection of related items e.g. days of th week or ingredients in a recipe. This page will illustrate the various HTML lists!

Ordered Lists

This is a collection of items in a particular order or steps that need to be followed or carried out in a given order. An example is given below

Days of the week.

  1. Sunday
  2. Monday
  3. Tuesday
  4. Wednesday
  5. Thursday
  6. Friday
  7. Saturday

Unordered Lists

This is a collection of items in no specific order or preference, though the items are related. An example is a list of ingredients to make KFC Chicken.

KFC Chicken Recipe:

    Ingredients
  • Unseasoned chicken (whatever you fancy)
  • Self raising flour
  • For the seasoning 5 cups of plain flour
  • 1/2 tbsp oregano
  • 1/2 tbsp thyme
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp mustard powder
  • 1 tbsp celery salt
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1/3 tbsp sea salt
  • 1 tbsp white pepper
  • 4 tbsp paprika
  • For the egg wash 1 egg
  • Dash of milk
